# HanFlow Framework

The HanFlow Framework presents a structured approach to embodied living, integrating movement, touch, and nourishment into a coherent system.

---

## Core Structure

HanFlow is built upon three interconnected domains:

- Movement (Tai Chi)
- Touch (Tuina)
- Nourishment (Mindful Eating)

These domains are not isolated practices, but mutually reinforcing aspects of a single embodied system.

---

## Principles

- Yielding over resistance  
- Balance over control  
- Attention over force  
- Rhythm over speed
